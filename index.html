ABOUT ME

I'm a Unity developer that trying to create immersive experience by making VR game. My days are filled with working on Unity, C#, and various other tools that allow me to bring virtual worlds to life. I spend hours refining gameplay mechanics, designing captivating environments, and implementing realistic interactions to ensure players are fully immersed in the VR experience.

I have worked on a variety of projects, including a virtual tour of the historic Haw Par Mansion, an interactive 3D model showcase for interior design, and a remake of the classic arcade game Buster Bros. I have also created a game called Lost Island, where players explore an abandoned island. Through these projects, I have developed skills in virtual reality, 3D modeling, game development, and more.
